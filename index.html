Our Mission

PathScan Technologies is a pioneering healthcare startup on a mission to democratize access to advanced digital pathology for labs of every size. By combining robust engineering with cost-effective automation, our innovative digital microscope platform transforms traditional slide analysis into an efficient, digital-first workflow.

Built with pathologist input and validated in local labs, our MVP offers whole-slide imaging, automated x-y stage control, and high clarity for accurate diagnostics. Whether in urban diagnostic centers or remote communities, PathScan empowers pathologists, caregivers, and patients with technology that bridges the accessibility gap.

Bridging the Gap

From urban diagnostic centers to remote communities, we're making advanced pathology accessible to everyone. Our technology ensures that location and budget never compromise the quality of diagnostic care.
